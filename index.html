Professional service — Free plan and quote!

Sunny's Electrical Services has over 5 years experience, providing various first-class electrical expertise. This includes working with businesses, local estate agents, housing authorities, landlords and many domestic customers!

Full rewiring

Removing previously old fittings and wiring, installing a new metal clad consumer unit and improving safety measures. Fully compliant with current regulations.

Part rewiring

Replacing a section of the initial installation: updating fuse boxes, fittings and cables. Upgrading to improve efficiency. Also, renewing sockets, lights and switches.

EICR Report

Electrical Installation Condition Report, referred to as a Landlord Safety Test or Homebuyer Test. Thorough assessment for domestic and commercial premises.

General Repairs

Can be through fault-finding. Other services include CCTV, house alarms and new circuits (e.g. extensions). Superior audio/visual upgrades available too!
